Rachel A. Giguere-Johnson

Music Producer, Founder of KCP

Passion for music is in my blood. I was born in Louisianna and grew up in Georgia as well. Both are known for the culture of musical influence, and it passed onto me! I never really thought of a career in music, as I never really wanted to be the front runner/Artist on stage. Around 2018, with the help of my wife, I found my calling in how I could be within the music business. Music Production has been the greatest gift I could of pursued and it has taken me far from my cooking days.
